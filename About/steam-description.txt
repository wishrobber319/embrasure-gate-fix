[b]Gates that actually seal against embrasures.[/b] Multi-tile doors and gates, including Vanilla Factions Expanded - Medieval 2's castle gates, refuse to close against see-through embrasure walls. This makes any embrasure count as a proper connection so your gates seal cleanly, without losing the shoot-through slots.

[hr][/hr]
[b]What's inside[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Gates connect to embrasures.[/b] Castle gates and other multi-tile doors treat embrasure walls as a valid connection, so they stop breaking at the seam.
[*][b]Works with any embrasure mod.[/b] Medieval Overhaul, Fortifications - Medieval and others are all covered.
[*][b]Slots still shoot through.[/b] The fix keeps embrasures fully usable as firing positions.
[/list]
